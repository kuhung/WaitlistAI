# WaitlistAI Promo Video - Storyboard & Script

## Theme / 主题思想

**EN**: WaitlistAI is the world's first AI product where the waitlist IS the feature. This promo video satirizes the absurdity of AI hype culture — infinite waitlists, buzzword-laden features, and the paradox of needing access to get access. The tone is dry humor meets cyberpunk aesthetics, a love letter to everyone who's ever been stuck on an AI product waitlist.

**ZH**: WaitlistAI 是全球首个"等待名单本身就是产品"的 AI 产品。本宣发视频讽刺 AI 炒作文化的荒诞 —— 无尽的等待名单、堆砌的技术术语，以及"需要邀请码才能申请邀请码"的悖论。基调是冷幽默与赛博朋克美学的结合，献给所有曾被 AI 产品等待名单困住的人。

## Scene Breakdown / 分镜拆解

### Scene 1: The Reveal (0s - 5s, 150 frames)

**Visual**: Pure black screen. A single terminal cursor blinks. Glitch text flickers: "AGI-Ready", "Quantum-Aligned", "Currently Unavailable". Each buzzword flashes and dissolves. Then the WaitlistAI logo slams into center with a spring animation. Tagline fades in below.

**Text EN**: `WaitlistAI` → `The AI That Waits For You`
**Text ZH**: `WaitlistAI` → `为你等待的 AI`

**Animation**: Glitch flicker → spring bounce logo entrance → fade-in tagline

---

### Scene 2: The Hype (5s - 12s, 210 frames)

**Visual**: Rapid-fire feature cards fly in from different directions. Each card has a neon icon, a buzzword title, and an absurd one-liner. Cards stack, overlap, and cascade. Counter at bottom: "1,247,893 developers are already waiting".

**Features (cycle through 6)**:
1. `Quantum-Aligned Processing` / `量子对齐处理` — "Results may vary by timeline"
2. `AGI-Ready Architecture` / `AGI就绪架构` — "Once someone figures out what that means"
3. `Zero-Latency Hallucinations` / `零延迟幻觉` — "Wrong answers, faster than ever"
4. `Infinite Context Window` / `无限上下文窗口` — "We just can't access most of it"
5. `Neural Pathway Optimization` / `神经通路优化` — "Side effects: existential dread"
6. `Blockchain-Verified Tokens` / `区块链验证Token` — "Investors love it"

**Animation**: Slide-in from alternating directions → stagger delay → scale pulse on each card

---

### Scene 3: The Paradox (12s - 19s, 210 frames)

**Visual**: A sleek modal appears: "Enter Your Invite Code". A cursor types a fake code. Error message glitches in: "This invite code belongs to a parallel universe." User clicks "Don't have a code? Request one →". New prompt: "To request an invite code, please enter your referral invite code." Zoom into the infinite loop. Text: "You need a code to get a code."

**Text EN**: `You need an invite code to get an invite code.` → `How peculiar.`
**Text ZH**: `你需要一个邀请码才能获得邀请码。` → `多么奇妙。`

**Animation**: Modal slide-up → typewriter input → glitch error → loop zoom effect

---

### Scene 4: The CAPTCHA (19s - 26s, 210 frames)

**Visual**: Cyberpunk-styled CAPTCHA interface. Step 1: A 3x3 grid appears — "Select all squares containing AGI". User clicks random squares. Verdict: "No images contained AGI. AGI does not exist yet." Step 2 flashes: "Prove you are not an AI". Step 3: "This statement is false — True or False?" Both answers marked wrong.

**Text EN**: `Verification Failed` → `This is by design. Welcome to AI ethics.`
**Text ZH**: `验证失败` → `这是设计如此。欢迎来到 AI 伦理。`

**Animation**: Grid appear → cell highlight pulse → stamp "FAILED" → fade transition

---

### Scene 5: The Queue (26s - 33s, 210 frames)

**Visual**: Queue position display, large number centered. Starts at #10,245. Number rapidly INCREASES (not decreases). Log messages scroll: "A Tier-3 Quantum Subscriber has been prioritized above you." Estimated wait time escalates: 3 hours → 2 days → 3 weeks → 6 months → 1 geological epoch → ∞.

**Text EN**: `Your position is going backwards.` → `Estimated wait: ∞`
**Text ZH**: `你的位置在倒退。` → `预计等待：∞`

**Animation**: Number counter animation (going up) → scrolling log → wait time escalation with scale emphasis

---

### Scene 6: The Verdict (33s - 40s, 210 frames)

**Visual**: An "AI Compatibility Report" card slides in. Metrics animate with progress bars:
- AI Compatibility Index: 12%
- Prompt Engineering Quotient: 3%
- Compute Worthiness Score: 0.7%
- Vibe Alignment Factor: 41%
- Hype Susceptibility Index: 98%

Final verdict pulses: "Please try again in 2-3 business eternities."

**Text EN**: `Recommendation: You are not eligible.` → `Try again in 2-3 business eternities.`
**Text ZH**: `建议：你不符合条件。` → `请在 2-3 个工作永恒后重试。`

**Animation**: Card slide-in → progress bar fill animation → verdict stamp

---

### Scene 7: The CTA (40s - 45s, 150 frames)

**Visual**: Screen clears to dark. WaitlistAI logo pulses with a subtle glow. Tagline types out character by character. URL fades in below. A small "v0.0.1-pre-alpha-nightly-unstable" badge appears in corner.

**Text EN**: `Join the waitlist. Or don't.` → `You'll be waiting either way.` → `waitlist-ai.vercel.app`
**Text ZH**: `加入等待名单。或者不加。` → `反正你都得等。` → `waitlist-ai.vercel.app`

**Animation**: Logo spring pulse → typewriter tagline → fade-in URL
